About Me

I am a passionate 3D artist and designer with expertise in Blender, Unity, and UI/UX design. I specialize in architectural modeling, environmental modeling, and game-ready assets.

My FYP, Zombie Escape, is a third-person survival game set in Karachi, where the city is under a zombie outbreak. Players must complete objectives to save hostages while managing resources like guns, bullets, and a health bar. The game features realistic environments modeled after iconic Karachi landmarks, including Empress Market, Shaheen Complex, Quaid-e-Azam Tomb, and Dolmen Mall, providing an immersive gameplay experience.
